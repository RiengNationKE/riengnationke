WE ARE THE STREETS

RiengnationKE is a Gengetone promotional channel based in Nairobi, Kenya.
We provide the latest videos, events, and industry news from the Gengetone music genre. Both the fans and aspiring artists, video directors, audio producers, DJs, and video vixens now have a platform to get involved in the community as a way of promoting the Gengetone style of music.
Whether you are new to the genre and looking to explore its musical offerings or a dedicated listener seeking more for your daily industry fix, RiengnationKE provides unparalleled coverge of all aspects of the genre.
